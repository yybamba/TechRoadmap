About

I am an AI platform architect and fintech infrastructure builder who has designed and built a multi-tenant financial infrastructure platform spanning five markets, integrating mobile money networks, cross-border payment corridors, PayFac models, BNPL engines, and AI-native tooling, all within a white-label, event-driven monorepo architecture.

I've also incorporated a multi-country holding structure and built the legal, compliance, and technical scaffolding most fintech teams take years and large budgets to assemble. That platform is Teddoh.

TechRoadmap is where I write about everything I've had to figure out — the architecture, the decisions, the market-specific constraints, and the honest realities of building as a founder-architect across multiple regulatory environments.
